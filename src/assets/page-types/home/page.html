Additional information

  1. To participate in the Refer a Friend scheme, both Referrer and Friend must follow the instructions on our website, britishgas.co.uk. A Referrer must successfully identify as an existing British Gas home energy or services customer via their online account. A Friend is an individual the Referrer knows personally, who isn’t already our customer and who is happy to receive the Referrer’s Referral link.

    To qualify for a Refer a Friend Amazon.co.uk Gift Card, as a Friend you must use the Referrer’s unique Referral link when joining British Gas on a Qualifying Product via britishgas.co.uk. The gift card is subject to the Friend’s Qualifying Product purchase and the Friend’s contract or policy must be active at least 60 days after the supply start date or the policy start date. The gift card Reward may take up to three months to arrive from the Friend joining British Gas.

    To qualify for a Reward for switching energy to British Gas, your Friend must not have held a British Gas energy contract within the last three months of joining. To qualify for a Reward for joining services, your Friend must not have held a British Gas services account within the last three months of joining. If after making a Qualifying Purchase your Friend cancels their agreement with us up to 60 days after the supply start date or the policy start date, then this will result in the cancellation of the Reward for the Friend and Referrer. We also require a valid email address to send you your gift card.

    Restrictions apply, see www.amazon.co.uk/gc-legal. Full terms and conditions (PDF, 276 KB) of the Refer a Friend scheme are also available to view.
