## Overview

<CompDetails name={componentName} />

British Gas creates many videos to engage customers. The `ns-video` component enables the use of these videos on the website and gives better context to the customer regarding what, how or why they should do something.

### Examples

<ExampleViewer type="standard" />

## Guidance

:::do
- Use videos from Centrica YouTube channels.
- Use inside another component (see [placement](/components/ns-video#placement)).
- Use as supplementary content.
:::

:::dont
- Use over three videos on a page.
- Use video as the primary content.
:::
